Safety

At Chevcom, safety is our top priority. We are committed to ensuring the well-being of our team members, clients, and all visitors to our construction sites. To meet this commitment, we adhere to all relevant safety regulations, including head protection requirements.

As per local laws and regulations, we require all personnel on our construction sites to wear appropriate head protection, typically in the form of hard hats. This is to reduce the risk of head injuries from falling objects or other potential hazards commonly found on construction sites.

We provide high-quality, well-maintained hard hats to all workers and ensure they are properly trained in their use. Our safety team conducts regular inspections to guarantee that head protection equipment is in good condition and that safety protocols are followed diligently.

Your safety is our priority, and we take every measure to create a secure working environment at our construction sites. If you have any questions or concerns about safety, please don't hesitate to reach out to our safety team.

At Chevcom, we prioritize the safety and well-being of our team members and visitors on our construction sites. To ensure a secure working environment, we adhere to strict first aid requirements, including:

  1. First Aid Kits: Well-stocked first aid kits are readily available at all our construction sites, equipped with essential supplies to address common injuries and medical emergencies.
  2. Trained First Aid Personnel: We designate and train specific personnel in first aid and CPR. These trained individuals are always present to provide immediate assistance when needed.
  3. Emergency Response Plan: Each site has a comprehensive emergency response plan that outlines the steps to follow in case of injuries or medical emergencies. This plan includes procedures for reporting incidents and summoning emergency medical services.
  4. Communication Systems: Effective communication systems, such as cell phones and radios, are in place to quickly contact emergency services and coordinate responses.
  5. Site Map: An up-to-date site map is posted and marked with the location of the first aid station, emergency exits, and assembly points for easy reference.
  6. Eye Wash Stations and Emergency Showers: For sites dealing with hazardous materials, we provide eye wash stations and emergency showers to address chemical exposures effectively.
  7. Training and Awareness: We ensure that all workers are aware of the location of first aid supplies, and they receive basic first aid and safety training.
  8. Medical Records and Reporting: We maintain accurate records of any incidents and injuries, reporting them as required by local regulations.
  9. Safety Signage: Clear and visible signage marks the locations of first aid stations and emergency equipment, aiding workers in quickly locating them when necessary.
  10. Regular Inspections: Routine inspections of first aid kits and equipment are conducted to ensure they are fully stocked, well-maintained, and within their expiration dates.

Your safety is our top priority, and we are committed to providing a secure environment on all our construction sites. If you have any questions or concerns regarding safety or first aid, please don't hesitate to contact our safety team for assistance.
